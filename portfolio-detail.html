Project Details

TalkyDuck is a smart home innovation that combines smart technology with simplicity. Inspired by the agility and calmness of ducks, this device is designed to respond to your voice quickly and accurately. The name TalkyDuck combines the ability to talk (Talky) and the adaptability of ducks (Duck). TalkyDuck brings technology that is easily accessible and blends with your lifestyle.
