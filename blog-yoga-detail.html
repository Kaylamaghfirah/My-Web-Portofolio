Taking My First Yoga Class Was One of the Best Decisions I Made

Kayla Maghfirah
August 09, 2024

During my semester break, I wanted to try something new and refreshing. That's when I decided to take my first-ever yoga class at Higar Studio.At first, I wasn't sure what to expect, but looking back, it turned out to be one of the best decisions I've ever made!

The moment I stepped into the studio, I felt a sense of calmness. The instructor warmly welcomed us and guided us through simple breathing exercises before moving into basic yoga poses. Though some poses were challenging, I enjoyed every moment. It helped me relax, improve my flexibility, and feel more connected to my body.

What I loved most about the class was how it taught me to slow down and focus on the present. The peaceful atmosphere, combined with the gentle flow of movements, made me feel refreshed and energized. By the end of the session, I felt lighter both physically and mentally.
